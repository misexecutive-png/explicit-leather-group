Our History

Born in Faridabad,
Known Worldwide

Explicit Leather Group was established in 1985 in Faridabad, India — the heart of India's leather manufacturing belt. Starting with a focused vision for quality leather garments, the group rapidly grew into a multi-division enterprise serving global fashion markets.

As a Recognised Export House under the Council of Leather Exports (Govt. of India), we pioneered the "used", "oiled", and "retro" leather looks — finishes that were revolutionary at the time and remain en vogue over 12 years later.

Today, the group spans four specialised companies: Explicit Leathers, Exquisite Enterprises, Gee & Gee Industries, and Scorpius Enterprises, each a leader in its domain.

Our Journey

1985
The Beginning
Explicit Leathers founded in Faridabad with a vision for premium leather garments for export markets.
1995
Export Recognition
Granted status of Recognised Export House by the Council of Leather Exports, Government of India.
2000
Pioneer Finishes
Launched pioneering "used", "oiled" and "retro" garment-washed leather finishes — revolutionising the industry.
2005
Group Expansion
Exquisite Enterprises and Scorpius Enterprises established, diversifying into goods, accessories & tanning.
2012
Gee & Gee Industries
Fourth arm established to handle tannery operations, bringing combined production capacity to 84 lakh sq ft/year.
2025
Global Presence
Operating with 4 companies, 2.1M sq ft production, serving fashion buyers across Europe, Americas & Asia.
