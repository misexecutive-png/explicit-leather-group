Explicit Leathers
The flagship enterprise of the group

The original and flagship enterprise of the Explicit Leather Group. Founded in 1990, Explicit Leathers is a leather goods and garments manufacturing firm and a Recognised Export House under the Council of Leather Exports, Government of India.

Explicit are world-renowned for their Garment Washed and Garment Dyed products, having pioneered the "used", "oiled", and "retro" looks which remain in vogue globally after 12+ years.

Gee & Gee Industries
Tannery operations & finished leather production

Gee & Gee Industries is the tannery backbone of the Explicit Leather Group. Their operations contribute to the group's combined production capacity of 84 Lakh sq ft per year — one of the largest in the region.

The company specialises in finishing and processing raw hides into premium finished leathers that supply both the group's own manufacturing units and external buyers globally.
