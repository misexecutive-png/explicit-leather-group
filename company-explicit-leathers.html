About Explicit Leathers

The Original
Export House

Founded in 1985 in Faridabad, Explicit Leathers is a Recognised Export House under the Council of Leather Exports, Government of India — the flagship enterprise of the group.

World-renowned for pioneering Garment Washed and Garment Dyed leather products — the 'used', 'oiled' and 'retro' looks that remain in vogue globally after 30+ years.
