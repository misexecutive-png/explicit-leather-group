About Exquisite Enterprises

The Group's
Tannery

Exquisite Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. is a sister concern owned by the promoters — a leather manufacturing tannery set up in 1984 in Faridabad, Haryana, on the outskirts of Delhi. Approximately 6,000 skins per day, per shift, are presently processed from the wet blue stage to finished leather.

The tannery is located within a 10-minute drive of the export unit and is used as the kitchen for new development of leather. It specializes in North Indian wool sheep skins, Kashmir sheep, goat, and cowcalf, with the expertise to make products in English Domestic, Middle Eastern, New Zealand and Spanish Enterfinos leathers, as well as African origin skins. At any given time, the tannery holds a stock of more than 3,00,000 skins.
