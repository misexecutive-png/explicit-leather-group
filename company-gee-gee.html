About Gee & Gee Industries

Tannery
Backbone

Gee & Gee Industries is a tannery also owned by Mr H.S.Bali manufacturing wet blue skins to finished leathers.The tannery also exports as finished leather various products to Italy/Turkey and China .The capacty of this tannery 6000 skins per days per shift. — one of the largest in the region.

The company specialises in finishing and processing raw hides into premium finished leathers that supply both the group's own manufacturing units and external buyers globally.
