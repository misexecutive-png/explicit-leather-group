About Scorpius Enterprises

Crafted
Leather Bags

The Explicit Group has also ventured into leather bags. Scorpius Enterprises is ably handled by Mrs. Jatin Bali and caters to both the international and domestic market.

Scorpius comprises a well-trained and hardworking team, all dedicated to their jobs with high quality standards and customer satisfaction as their main goal. We make leather bags for both men and women in any style, colour, quality and finish — with a deep fashion sense, we are well equipped to cater to the highly dynamic and competitive market of leather bags. Our specialty is giving a retro, vintage, distressed, oiled as well as washed look. Hand finishing and dip dyeing are also our forte.
